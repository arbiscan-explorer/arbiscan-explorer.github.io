Arbiscan: Exploring the Arbitrum Blockchain with Transparency and Precision

As blockchain ecosystems grow in complexity and scale, reliable tools for tracking on-chain activity become essential. Arbiscan is the leading blockchain explorer for Arbitrum, a Layer 2 scaling solution built on Ethereum. Designed to provide transparency, accessibility, and detailed insights into blockchain data, Arbiscan plays a critical role for developers, traders, researchers, and everyday users interacting with the Arbitrum network.

What Is Arbiscan?

Arbiscan is a block explorer and analytics platform that allows users to view, search, and analyze transactions, smart contracts, addresses, and blocks on the Arbitrum network. It is developed and maintained by the same team behind Etherscan, Ethereum’s most widely used block explorer, ensuring familiarity, reliability, and robust performance.

Arbiscan supports multiple Arbitrum networks, including:

Arbitrum One (mainnet)

Arbitrum Nova

Arbitrum testnets

Why Arbitrum Needs Arbiscan

Arbitrum uses Optimistic Rollup technology to improve Ethereum’s scalability by:

Reducing transaction fees

Increasing throughput

Maintaining Ethereum-level security

Because transactions are executed off-chain and later settled on Ethereum, users need a specialized explorer to track this activity. Arbiscan fills this gap by translating complex Layer 2 data into readable, verifiable information.
